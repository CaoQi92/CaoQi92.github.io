    About Me

    Hello, I’m Qi Cao (曹婍), an associate professor at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I recerved my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Prof. Guojie Li and Prof. Huawei Shen in 2020. Before that, I received my B.S. degree from Renmin University of China in 2015.

    Research Interest

    • Trustworthy Recommender Systems
      • Robustness and fairness in RS
      • Algorithm auditing in RS
    • Social Media Computing and Mining
      • Information diffusion prediction
      • Network representation learning
